About Us

We're business owners too. We know what it's like to work every hour going. That's why we built this.

Why We Do This

Because too many smart business owners are stuck doing work that a machine could do for them. We're here to give you that time back — so you can spend it where it actually matters.

What We Believe

Technology should work for you, not the other way round. You shouldn't need a computer science degree to make your business run better. If we can't explain it in plain English, we haven't understood it well enough ourselves.

How We Work

Straight talking, no nonsense, and we always do what we say we'll do. We treat your business like it's our own, because we know what it means to build something from scratch.

Our Story

We started MoreTimeOff.work because we kept meeting brilliant business owners — people who'd built something real from nothing — who were drowning in admin, paperwork, and repetitive tasks. They were working 60-hour weeks when they should have been enjoying the rewards of everything they'd built.

We've spent over 20 years working with technology in some of the UK's most demanding industries. We know what works, what doesn't, and — most importantly — how to make things simple enough that anyone can use them. We don't do complicated.

Every business we work with gets the same thing: a straight conversation, an honest assessment, and a clear plan. If we can't save you time or money, we'll tell you. And if we can, we'll show you exactly how — then get it done.
